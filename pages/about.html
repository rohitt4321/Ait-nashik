About Us

AIT is an educational wing of Access – Group. For over three decades, AIT is a leading training Center in North Maharashtra for Mechanical – Civil – Electrical & Robotics Automation Design Engineering courses in the field of CAD CAM CAE. We also provide Placement assistance for the successful students, to be an integral part of growing ER&D teams. Access CADD provides job oriented courses with 100% placement assistance to the students design domain. Till date Access CADD has placed more than 20000 + Engineering students in leading R&D in India.

Mission
We want to become a globally respected training institution providing skilled CAD professionals for ER&D and Steel Detailing industries. Delivered by a highly capable, experienced and passionate trainer.

Values
Knowledge sharing our mantra. We believe sharing our knowledge is a way to achieve immortality. We aim to enrich individuals, create successful professionals & careers for our students.

Quality
Teaching is responsibility
We at AIT, believe teaching is Not Just A Profession for earning, it’s a responsibility to be a part in shaping the future of our engineering students.
