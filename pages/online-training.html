Online Training

Access CADD  provides online self-paced training for engineers. The training course of Access CADD contains courses Library and tutorials for today's leading design software and industry topics. This Online Training allows you to overcome the typical challenges of traditional training 

Key Features of Online Training

  •  
  • Top-Quality Video Streaming Compelling live classes with concepts, live projects, charts and graphics to make it convenient in learning to the learners.
  • Courseware and Practice Tutorials Get the latest updated with Training Materials. The course contents meet the industry standard based on the latest technologies
  • Experienced Instructors, those are experienced professionals in their fields, and use the unique method to teach the students with a passion for sharing their expertise.
  • Learning with Projects Students are given the opportunity to work with projects in different categories in their respective fields. It helps the students to learn with the right tools and knowledge for grabbing design opportunity.
  • Career Opportunity In design filed , students get career plan and career navigation path, by including the development at the personal and professional level which helps students to maximize their potential.
  • Certificates of Completion to present yourself at the professional level or to show potential employers what you’ve accomplished
